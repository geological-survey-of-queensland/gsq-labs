GSQ Labs (beta)


A series of geoscience related web tools managed by the Geological Survey of Queensland (GSQ) on GitHub.

These tools are often early stage products. They are offered under disclaimer and under continuous development. Feedback to geological_info@resources.qld.gov.au is appreciated.

  • OCR Search Tool

    Optical character recognition has been carried out on 60K+ industry reports. The text from this process has been added to a search engine and been made available for searching
